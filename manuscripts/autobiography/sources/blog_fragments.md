## THE TWO MOTHERLODE POSTS

### M1: "I wrote this to A friend but its really a letter to everyone" (28 April 2011)

**Path:** .../2011/04/28/i-wrote-this-to-a-friend-.../index.html
**The single most complete autobiographical document.** Written 13 days after the first blog post. Confessional first person. Covers Chapters 4-8.

> "I was in the property Business I had a Residential and Commercial Estate Agency/consultancy in London Docklands and A group of development companies which I disposed of between 2000 and 2005. I built up my business from 1990 to 2000 and concentrated solely on my development and investment companies from 1997 to 2003. I always kept my teams very small... my sole objective was very narrow in that I wanted solely to make Money and get out. I really didn't enjoy the experience It made me very selfish and ultimately very ill although Financially it made me independent. My Business career showed me the worst of human greed in myself and also in my business partners investors..."

> "When I sold my main company in 2003 my world fell apart My wife and I divorced I became a drug addicted alcoholic I spent 2 years more or less trying to self destruct I attempted suicide and did all I could to self destruct it was a very lonely time and if not for my wealth at that time I think I would have sunk without trace."

> "When I became a father I cleaned up and found a sense of purpose and responsibility I had never felt before and since 2005 when my Daughter was born I have become the person I always was inside."

> "I was in therapy privately for over ten years which greatly limited the damage which my bi polar could ultimately have wrought upon my life."

**Voice:** Witness. **Chapters:** 4 (Docklands), 5 (Breakdown), 6 (Fatherhood), 7 (Estate loss).

### M2: "Lords, A personal reaction to Brino" (9 February 2019)

**Path:** .../2019/02/09/lords-a-personal-reaction-to-brino-.../index.html
**The most detailed childhood/youth narrative.** Covers Chapters 1-8.

> "I spent the formative years of my life in Oxfordshire. I went to the Warriner school Bloxham..."

> "I was brought up in a small village called Barford St. Michael and just outside the village is a nice house at which, as a young man I was the gardener for Mrs Stapleton. [...] I conceived of a dream and set myself the task that I wanted to buy that house [...] I never did buy Rignall Hall but what I did do, is I bought the Belmont Estate near Bristol which adjoins Tyntesfield and became an eccentric country squire"

> "My First Novel the Plantsman is about my journey from a teenaged dreamer shovelling gravel on Mrs Stapleton's Drive"

> "my family moved to West Germany, as it was then. My father worked for the government and among other things he looked after the training ranges at Saltau"

> "that last summer in Oxfordshire was a formative summer for me, it was the summer of the Bristol and Brixton Riots [...] I attended the first Fairport Convention concert"

> "my first wife heard me speak was reading a passage... from the famous book about an Oxfordshire childhood, Cider with Rosie. My five years in Oxfordshire gave me an Oxfordshire accent"

> "playing village cricket that last summer back in 1980 I remember playing Sibford Gower. [...] After the match, we went to the bishops Blais pub"

> "It was a dream which sustained me through deep suicidal depressions and Manic episodes provoked by self-medicating on Alcohol and strippers, after my divorce I found Strippers were a gateway drug to the harder stuff [...] becoming a Millionaire by the age of Thirty and retiring at the Age of 38 with a personal fortune in the High single millions. And then 2008 happened."

> "By 2012 My Mansion on my Country Estate & The Estates Lands had been sold by Law of property act receivers."

**Voice:** Witness. **Chapters:** 2 (Barford), 3 (Germany), 4 (Career), 5 (Breakdown), 7 (Estate loss), 8 (Sweden).
