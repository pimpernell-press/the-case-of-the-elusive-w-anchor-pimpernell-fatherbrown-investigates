# Preface

[PLACEHOLDER — Replace before publication.]

This novella began as a complaint about a stolen domain name and ended as an investigation into the structure of money itself. The journey between those two points is the story.

Father Brown's method — empathy, not deduction — proved to be the only instrument capable of navigating the layers of misdirection, regulatory capture, and institutional indifference that the investigation uncovered. He does not deduce the criminal's identity from evidence. He imagines himself as the criminal, and from inside that imagination, the evidence arranges itself.

The institutions described in this book are real. The crimes are structural. The pigeon is unsanctioned.
