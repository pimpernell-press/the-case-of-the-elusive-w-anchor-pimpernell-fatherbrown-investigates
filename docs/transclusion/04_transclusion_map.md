## Part 3: The Bridge — P09 (The Generational Spine)

**P09 is the closest approach and the anchor point for the Incomplete Theodolite.**

The passage in ch010:30-70 contains:

| Element | Track B (Biography) | Track D (Evidence) |
|---------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Foster Lewis (b.1902) | "Miner, witness. What does labour cost in a body?" | The Miner's Tale as primary source document |
| Roger Lewis (b.~1965) | "Developer, poet, blogger. What does capital cost in a life?" | Conquest of Dough, FAIR-Index as analytical output |
| The Reader (now) | "Citizen, debtor. What does AI cost in a truth?" | Clockwork Forest as fable |
| Check-weighing | "The colliery had two weights." Wage fraud as lived experience. | Deuteronomy 25:13-16 as regulatory text. The check-weighman as elected auditor — proto-FAIR-Index. |
| Chapter titles | "The Moleskin Trousers" — material culture | "The Struggle for the Minimum Wage" — economic data |
| The Miner's Tale | Grandfather's memoir. The body underground. | Historical evidence of extractive labour economics. |

**This passage IS the bridge.** It is the only place in the 55-chapter
corpus where:
1. A named person (Foster Lewis) IS a data point
2. A biographical narrative (miner's memoir) IS evidence
3. A family history (grandfather → grandson) IS an analytical framework
4. A physical experience (the body underground) IS an economic measurement

The Incomplete Theodolite's Chapter 1 must start here.

## Summary: What Stage 04 Produces for Stage 05

1. **24 key passages identified** across the 55-chapter corpus, mapped to
   all four tracks with overlap analysis.

2. **The B↔D gap is compositional, not conceptual.** The material exists
   on both sides. Nobody has written the connecting sentence.

3. **P09 (ch010:30-70) is the anchor point.** The generational spine
   (Foster Lewis → Roger Lewis → The Reader) is where biography and
   evidence come closest. The check-weighman IS the proto-FAIR-Index.

4. **Five passages must be written new** (N01-N05) for the Incomplete
   Theodolite Chapter 1. Everything else exists.

5. **ch001 Hand Shandy is the Rosetta Stone** — every section serves
   2-3 tracks. It is the map of the territory but not the bridge.

6. **The bridge is the Incomplete Theodolite.** It attaches to P09
   and extends the biographical register into the evidential register
   by giving the elegy a number and the number a name.
