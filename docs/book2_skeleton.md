## The Argument

The COPE Brain is not a research corpus. It is an operational map.
Every node is a supplier, a component, a process, a tool, or a
decision point in the machine that turns land into homes without
intermediaries. The 98 "orphan" nodes from the novel cross-reference
are not orphans. They are the operational content for Book 2.

The book shows the machine. The novel (Book 1) makes the case for
why the machine is needed. The autobiography (Book 3) shows who
built it and what it cost him. Book 2 is the middle volume — the
one the investor reads, the builder uses, and the regulator audits.
